Roles Selection Primer

These rules are to select roles, the core of Throne Magic.

There are three ways to select roles.

  1. Basic selection: The King, the Usuerper, and their respective Bannerman are used. If there is an between 5 and 7 people playing, use the Dragon, the Barbarian, or the Wizard (respectively) as well.
  2. Drafting: The players draft the roles according to the rules presented here.
  3. Random: Roles are chosen according to drafting method, but all roles are chosen randomly from the pool currently available.
