Welcome to Throne Magic

Throne Magic is a casual variant where you assume the role of a medieval character in an epic power struggle against your mortal enemies! Play as The King, The Userper's Bannermen, or even the Dragon!

A Magic: The Gathering variant created by the now-defunct Magic Lampoon. Still viewable on the Wayback Machine!
